Rijoy Mukherjee

I am a Ph.D. student in the Dept of CSE at IIT Kharagpur, advised by Prof. Rajat Subhra Chakraborty. I am funded by the Prime Minister Research Fellowship (PMRF) scheme from May 2020. My research interests include exploring Large Language Models (LLMs) for all aspects of CAD and EDA workflows with the aim to integrate LLMs for various tasks, such as designing or verification of hardware. My other research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, hardware security, and cryptography, specifically exploring hardware implementation-specific vulnerabilities of ML accelerators and developing robust, low-cost, deployable solutions for them.
